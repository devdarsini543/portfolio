Multifunction Engine Control System

This project is an intelligent solution designed to enhance vehicle safety by autonomously monitoring and responding to critical driving conditions. It detects if the driver is under the influence of alcohol and, if so, automatically disables the engine and initiates parking. The system also checks for signs of driver drowsiness and triggers an alert; if there's no response within a few seconds, it safely parks the vehicle to prevent potential accidents. Additionally, it maintains a safe distance from obstacles by adjusting the vehicle’s speed when necessary. By integrating real-time monitoring and automated control actions, this system makes driving more efficient and safe.
